ContentFloww - Refund Policy

Last updated: 29/09/2023

Thank you for choosing ContentFloww.. We appreciate your business and trust. This document outlines our policy regarding refunds.

a. General: At ContentFloww, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds for any products or services purchased from us.‍

b. Exceptions: There are no exceptions to this policy unless explicitly stated in a written agreement signed by both the customer and ContentFloww.

2. Quality Assurance‍

While we do not offer refunds, we stand by the quality of our products and services. If you encounter any issues or have concerns, please contact our customer service team, and we will do our best to assist you.‍
