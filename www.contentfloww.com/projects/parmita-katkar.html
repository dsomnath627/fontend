Parmita Katkar

35 Million Impressions, 240,000 new followers, and thousands of dollars in sales in just 90 days

Mission & Challenge

Parmita was investing a lot of resources in creating long-form YouTube videos but had no solid short-form content strategy

She was only relying on YouTube videos to grow and monetize her brand but with every platform pushing short-form videos, she was struggling to grow her brand and monetize the audience.

She wanted a solid video team that could:

  1. Create and post short-form content on all her social channels
  2. Grow her brand on all main platforms simultaneously
  3. Create a content funnel to monetize her audience organically
  4. Create Automation for lead generation

When we started working with her in March 2023:

The mission was to leverage the power of short-form content to grow her brand on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook simultaneously.

Monetization was another important aspect that we decided to focus on as she had no strategy to convert even her existing followers into paying customers.

Outcome

Deliverables that not only grow her brand but make her thousands of dollars every month:

  1. Editing MOAT - An editing style that is unique to her brand and the type of content she creates
  2. Holistic short-form content strategy - A strategy that suits her way of recording the videos. The idea was to give actionable tips and inspire with every video we create and post
  3. Organic growth strategy - Putting together a publishing strategy to help her grow on TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook simultaneously.
  4. Monetization systems - We optimized CTA for every video to make sure we not only bring her new followers but also convert those followers into paying customers
  5. Automations - Instagram and Facebook DM automation for lead generation on autopilot

Results & Impacts

35 Million impressions across all social media platforms
240,000+ New overall followers
Multiple Viral videos
Multi-platform growth
Automation for lead generation
Thousands of dollars in revenue
